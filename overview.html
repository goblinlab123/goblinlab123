Overview

Here at the Goblin Laboratory, we are, predictably, in the business of making Goblins. Actually, we’re in the business of helping you make goblins.

Leveraging thousands of hours of illustration work from the last decade of Gossip Goblin’s career, we’ve created a delightfully simple drag and drop tool allowing you to assemble your very own Goblin NFT from a pool of random parts, all from the comfort of your living room.

Artwork

Spanning Earth’s primordial, dinosaur-infested origins, through our not-so-far-future as virtual brains in vats, to the sobering end of the universe as we know it, Gossip Goblin utilizes pens, paper, and a whole lot of tiny lines to explore the world of goblins.

And now, for the first time ever, you are cordially invited to add to this goblin-ridden journey by crafting a unique goblin of your very own.

Assembly

Step 1: Acquire a goblin brain.

Step 2: Visit the goblin lab and begin experimenting on your brain.

Step 3: Behold the randomized properties inside the brain. Drag and drop, resize, and manipulate the properties until your masterpiece is complete.

Step 4: Clout. Profit. Eternal Glory. Use your experiment as a mintpass to unlock and build future NFTs for free.

Tech

This project is an experiment to go beyond canned, cookie-cutter NFTs. We want you in the creation process.

You might be wondering "Wait, aren't my NFTs unique and permanent?" The answer: Not really. But they could be.

Each brain you mint serves as a ticket to interactively craft a unique goblin off-chain through our drag-and-drop interface.

The ensemble of parts composing each brain has a population-level rarity that we’ll tell you up front.

Once you’re done, we store your creation on the permaweb, and our smart contract updates your NFT’s on-chain metadata to point to it. We intend these to last.
